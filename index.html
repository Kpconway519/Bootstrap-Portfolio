About Me

My name is Kevin Conway and I am an aspiring web developer. I am currently enrolled in the Web Development Bootcamp at the University of North Carolina Charlotte. Once I complete this course, I will have the skills necessary to be a junior web developer.

In 2014, I graduated from Mississippi State University with a Bachelor's in History. I am currently employed at Williams Buick GMC as a Sales Professional. In total, I have been working in sales for almost three and a half years.

I feel that my background in sales as well as my education make me an ideal candidate for a web developer position. My history degree required a great deal of research and instilled in me powerful critical thinking and problem solving skills, an appreciation for proper documentation, and a recognition of the power of clear and concise presentation. As a salesman, I have to be persuasive, match my product with customers' needs, and collaborate with many different parties to structure a successful deal. In my spare time, I read sales books, record myself speaking, and otherwise work on improving my sales skills. I am always working on becoming a better person, and for that reason, as well as all the above reasons, I believe I would be an ideal web developer.
